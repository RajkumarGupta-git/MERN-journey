Spider Man

About

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazon Fantasy # 15 (August 1962) in the Silver Age Comic Books. He has been featured in comic books , television shows, films , video games, novels, and plays.
